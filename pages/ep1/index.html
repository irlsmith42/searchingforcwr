Designations was their underground hit. It was long—over 200,000 words, which translates to about 800 pages in a conventional book. And it was hard—it challenged the reader. Over the year-or-so span of 2013 to 2014, when it was posted, chapter by chapter, it gained a miniature cult following.

People loved this story. It had thousands of comments, and a little fanbase of its own. By 2015, Designations had an audiobook adaptation, a 2-hour radio play spinoff, and yes, a band. They had two albums and two EPs.
